Harrison Behind the Grill

Smoke rises from the grill, carrying laughter, quiet meals, and family warmth. The scent of inasal fills the air, proudly Filipino. It is the playful spirit of your kuya, making every bite feel like home. At Harrison, every skewer is a story, every table a gathering, every moment shared.

We welcome every appetite and every story. Whether you're craving comfort, connection, or just a quiet bite, Harrison is always open.
